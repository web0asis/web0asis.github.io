About

Flip text, letters, and words. Use this tool to turn texts upside down and make them harder to read. When backwards is enabled, it will flip the whole string. Toggle off backwards option to flip each line. The effect is reversible, which means you can convert regular text and upside down text back and forth.

Supported Characters

Both uppercase (A-Z) and lowercase (a-z) letters are supported, as well as numbers (0-9), punctuations and special characters.
