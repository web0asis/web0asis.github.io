What is your privacy policy? Do you keep logs? What data do you store?

  • All logs are deleted after 24 hours.
  • We do NOT record or keep track of any of your searches. We serve no ads, we have no tracking scripts, we have no analytics software.
  • Any cookies we store in your browser is for your benefit of remembering your custom settings. It has nothing to do with tracking.
  • The site is primarily made from just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We only store data that is absolutely crucial for site function.

What should I do if the page doesn't load correctly or there are dead links?

  • Please report all issues on our Forum. We will do our best to help you and solve the issue or bug.
  • To fix most issues with WebOasis, Please clear all cookies and cache from your web browser then restart your browser.
  • You also want to click the "Reset All To Default" button located in the upper left popout menu on the homepage of WebOasis.
  • This forces your browser to delete all cookies, cache, & localstorage related to WebOasis and re-download everything from our server.

How often do you add new links or remove broken links?

  • We try to do this at least once per week.
  • If you see no changes, clear your browser's cache and click the "Reset All To Default" button in the left popout menu on WebOasis.
